HOUR OF CODE

Another project of ours is called “Hour of Code”. In this project, we are planning to go to the Baku Turkish Lyceum of Turkey Diyanet Foundation and hold an "Hour of Code" event between 6 and 12 December. Our main mission is to introduce middle school students to the world of programming and deliver the message that everyone can learn how to code at any age independently. In the project, Mammad Mammadov and Raul Hatamov will give some information about ADA University, while Atilla Sharifli and Ibrahim Aliyev will give talk about computer science. Throughout the event, we are going to play colorful games with children and award winners at the end. All in all, teaching something to children is a glorious purpose, and we hope that we will successfully overcome this mission.
